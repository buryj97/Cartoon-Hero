Avatar Aang

About Aang

As the one person in the world who can master all four elements, the Avatar is responsible for maintaining balance. Aang is 12 going on 112, as he froze himself in an iceberg for 100 years to survive a storm. He has crazy adventures with his friends, his flying bison, and his lemur. Despite being just a kid, he masters all the elements in less than a year to defeat the the evil firelord.
